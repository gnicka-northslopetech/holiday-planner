Thu Aug 14 — Travel Day

London → Athens → Milos

FLIGHT London to Athens · 3h 30m direct Best price

  • BA / Aegean from Heathrow
  • easyJet from Gatwick
  • Ryanair from Stansted · Wizz Air from Luton
  • Expect ~£95–160 return

FLIGHT Athens to Milos · 40 min

  • Olympic Air from €44 / Sky Express from €39 Best price
  • August realistically €50–170 one way

FERRY ALT Piraeus to Milos · SeaJets high-speed ~2h 35m · €63–90

Athens crew: Same options — fly from ATH or ferry from Piraeus. Let's coordinate arrival times.

Aug 14–19 — Milos

5 nights · rent a car
  • Sarakiniko (lunar landscape beach)
  • Tsigrado & Firiplaka (south coast gems)
  • Kleftiko boat tour from Adamas (~€60–120pp)
  • Plaka village for sunset
  • Paleochori (hot sand beach with tavernas)
Heads up: Aug 15 is Assumption of Mary — big Greek holiday, expect crowds and some places closed.

Tue Aug 19 — Island Hop

Milos → Koufonisia

FERRY SeaJets (sole operator)

  • Departs Milos ~08:00
  • Arrives Koufonisia ~12:00–13:00
  • Duration: ~4 hours
  • Price: €107.70 per person
Important: This route only runs on certain days. Aug 19 (Tuesday) is confirmed as a sailing day.

Aug 19–21 — Koufonisia

2 nights · no car needed
  • Pori beach (best on the island, fine white sand)
  • Italida & Fanos beaches
  • Chora village — tavernas, bars, bakeries
  • Sea taxi around the island
  • Walk everywhere — island is tiny

Thu Aug 21 — Return

Koufonisia → Athens → London

FERRY Koufonisia to Piraeus

  • SeaJets Champion Jet 3
  • Departs ~13:20, arrives Piraeus ~17:50
  • €42–85 per person

TAXI Piraeus to Athens Airport · ~45 min · ~€40


FLIGHT Athens to London (evening)

  • 21:15 Ryanair → Stansted (safest timing, cheapest) Best price
  • 19:10 Aegean → Heathrow (tight but doable)
  • 20:45 easyJet → Luton (check if runs Fridays)
Safer option: Take the evening ferry on Aug 20 instead, sleep near Athens airport, fly out morning of Aug 21. Removes all stress.
Athens crew: You're home once you dock at Piraeus!
